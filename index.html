Try Together with Matias Curutchet

My name is Matias Curutchet, and I founded this organization with a mission to empower the youth of Costa Rica by enhancing their knowledge of financial and entrepreneurship literacy while promoting the sport of rugby. At the age of twelve, my family immigrated from Uruguay to Costa Rica due to financial difficulties stemming from poor investment decisions and mismanagement of business affairs. Throughout this challenging transition, rugby became my refuge, providing a strong sense of community and support. Driven by a deep commitment to offering young people an outlet during difficult times, while equipping them with the tools to avoid similar financial struggles, I established the Try Together Foundation to make a meaningful impact on future generations.

Why Try Together Foundation

QUALITY FINANCIAL LITERACY CAPACITATIONS

The Try Together Foundation teaches the youth of Costa Rica about savings practices, food stamps, credit card usage, bank accounts, basic banking, and taxes.

ENGLISH CLASSES

The Try Together Foundation tutors and teaches English from level A1 to level C2 for free. The classes consist of grammar for exams and English literature and composition for exams (AP, IB, TOEFL,etc). Additionally, we promote the acting of plays and oral presentations for English enrichment.

HEALTHY SNACKS

The Try Together Foundation provides filtered water, fresh juice, sports drinks, fruits, vegetables and more nutritious food for free to all its participants on Saturdays.

RUGBY TRAINING

The Try Together Foundation provides free rugby gear and training to all its participants so that they learn about exercising, discipline, and teamwork.

HEALTHY MIND, HEALTHY FINANACES, AND A HEALTHY BODY

The Try Together Foundation teaches the youth of Costa Rica about the importance of maintaining a healthy lifestyle and constant learning and studying habits, especially in finances and literacy. The Try Together Foundation is helping its participants pay debt, get better jobs, and aspire for college scholarships. Additionally, we teach members about healthy sleep, exercise, and learning routines based on scientific research available online.
