Why Choose Us

QUALITY FINANCIAL LITERACY TRAINING

The Try Together Foundation teaches the youth of Costa Rica about savings practices, food stamps, credit card usage, bank accounts, basic banking, and taxes.

ENGLISH CLASSES

The Try Together Foundation tutors and teaches English from level A1 to level C2 for free. The classes consist of grammar for exams and English literature and composition for exams (AP, IB, TOEFL,etc). Additionally, we promote the acting of plays and oral presentations for English enrichment.

HEALTHY SNACK

The Try Together Foundation provides filtered water, fresh juice, sports drinks, fruits, vegetables and more nutritious food for free to all its participants on Saturdays.

RUGBY TRAINING

The Try Together Foundation provides free rugby gear and training to all its participants so that they learn about exercising, discipline, and teamwork.
